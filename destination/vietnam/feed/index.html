Itinerary for 8 Nights 9 Days Vietnam Tour Package

DAY 1

Arrival & Hanoi Sightseeing

  • Arrival at Hanoi, the capital city of Vietnam, pickup & drive to your hotel.
  • Check-in on reaching your hotel & freshen up.
  • Visit Hoan Kiem Lake and Ngoc Son Temple.
  • Explore the Old Quarter with its narrow streets and traditional architecture.
  • Visit the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum and the One Pillar Pagoda.
  • Visit St Joseph Cathedral and Museum of Ethnology.
  • Overnight stay at Hanoi.

DAY 2

Ninh Binh Day Tour

  • Travel to Ninh Binh, the ancient town of Vietnam.
  • Head to Tam Coc and take a morning tour along the Ngo Dong River, enjoying the scenic karst landscapes and caves.
  • Visit Bich Dong Pagoda, exploring the three pagodas built into the mountainside.
  • Drive to Hoa Lu – the ancient capital and visit the temples of the Dinh and Le dynasties.
  • Return to Hanoi & rest for the night.

DAY 3

Overnight Cruise to Halong

  • After breakfast, checkout & proceed for your Lacasta Cruise to Halong.
  • Enjoy the 5 star cruise boating overnight on Lacasta.

DAY 4

Transfer to Hue

  • After lunch, checkout & drive to Hanoi airport for your drop.
  • Board flight for Hue, arrival at Hue & drive to your hotel & checkin.

DAY 5

Hue Sightseeing

  • After breakfast, proceed for Hue sightseeing.
  • Explore the historic Imperial City, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Visit the Thien Mu Pagoda and take a boat trip along the Perfume River.
  • Explore the ancient royal tombs, such as the Tomb of Khai Dinh and Tomb of Minh Mang.
  • Overnight stay at Hue.

DAY 6

Day Tour to Phong Nha caves

  • Reach the Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park, the home to some of the world’s largest and most beautiful caves.
  • Begin your adventure by taking a trip into the Phong Nha Cave.
  • Marvel at the stunning stalactite and stalagmite formations inside the cave.
  • Visit the Paradise Cave, another breathtaking cave in the national park.
  • Take a tour to explore the cave’s chambers and admire its impressive formations.
  • Return to Hue & rest for the night.

DAY 7

Drive to Da Nang via Hoi An

  • After breakfast, checkout & proceed to visit Marble Mountains.
  • Visit the Marble Mountains (Ngu Hanh Son): climb to the top for panoramic views and explore the caves and pagodas.
  • Drive to Hoi An, acharming ancient town with well-preserved architecture.
  • Wander through the Old Town, known for its lantern-lit streets.
  • Visit the Japanese Covered Bridge, Tan Ky Old House, and the Assembly Hall of the Fujian Chinese Congregation.
  • Enjoy a half day Basket Boat Tour at Coconut Village.
  • Drive to Da Nang & check-in at your hotel on reaching.
  • Overnight stay at Da Nang.

DAY 8

Bana Hills, Golden Bridge & Da Nang Sightseeing

  • Early morning, start your journey to Ba Na Hills.
  • Arrive at Ba Na Hills and take the cable car to the mountain. Enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes during the ride.
  • Explore the attractions at Ba Na Hills, including the French Village, Fantasy Park, Linh Ung Pagoda, and the Golden Bridge.
  • Visit additional attractions such as the Debay Wine Cellar and the Wax Museum.
  • Return to Da Nang & visit Han Market. Explore the market, try some local snacks, and shop for souvenirs.
  • Visit the Museum of Cham Sculpture to learn about the Cham civilization and its artistic history.
  • Head to the Dragon Bridge and take a stroll along the Han River, come back in the evening to witness the dragon breathing fire and water.
  • Explore the Da Nang Cathedral and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere.
  • Overnight stay at Da Nang.

DAY 9

Departure

  • Checkout & drive to Da Nang Airport for your drop.
