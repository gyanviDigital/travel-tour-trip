Itinerary for Our 5 Nights 6 Days Kashmir Tour Package

DAY 1

Arrival in Srinagar & Local sightseeing

➥ Arrive at Sheikh ul-Alam International Airport in Srinagar.

➥ Check in to your hotel or houseboat.

➥ After settling in and freshening up at your accommodation, take some time to relax and acclimate to your surroundings.

➥ Visit Mughal Gardens: Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi. Explore Shankaracharya Temple for panoramic views of Srinagar.

➥ Visit Hazratbal Shrine, a revered Muslim shrine on the northern shores of Dal Lake.

➥  Return to your hotel or houseboat for a delicious dinner featuring authentic Kashmiri cuisine, followed by a comfortable overnight stay.

DAY 2

Srinagar – Gulmarg Day Trip

➥ After a hearty breakfast, embark on an exhilarating day trip to Gulmarg, often referred to as the ‘Meadow of Flowers’. En route, stop at the Shankaracharya Temple, located atop a hill, to soak in panoramic views of Srinagar city.

➥ Upon reaching Gulmarg, hop on the Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world, offering breathtaking views of the snow-capped peaks and verdant valleys below. Ascend to the upper reaches of the mountain for unparalleled vistas and the opportunity to engage in snow-related activities (depending on the season).

➥ Spend the day exploring the picturesque meadows, dotted with colorful wildflowers during the warmer months or transformed into a winter wonderland during snowfall.

➥ You can also indulge in various optional activities such as horse riding, trekking, or simply enjoying a picnic amidst nature’s splendor.

➥ In the late afternoon, return to Srinagar and unwind with a sumptuous dinner at your hotel or houseboat, followed by a restful night’s sleep.

DAY 3

Srinagar – Sonmarg Day Trip

➥ After breakfast, embark on a picturesque drive to Sonmarg, a charming town located approximately 3 hours from Srinagar, known as the ‘Meadow of Gold’.

➥ Upon arrival, visit the renowned Thajiwas Glacier, a popular destination for trekking enthusiasts and those who love to revel in the snowy landscapes.

➥ Explore the lush meadows, take in the stunning mountain vistas, and enjoy a delightful pony ride through the scenic trails.

➥ In the late afternoon, make your way back to Srinagar, taking with you the unforgettable memories of Sonmarg’s natural splendor.

DAY 4

Srinagar – Pahalgam Day Trip

➥ After a hearty breakfast, embark on a scenic drive to Pahalgam, a journey of approximately 2-3 hours from Srinagar. Marvel at the breathtaking views along the way as you leave the bustling city behind and enter the tranquil ‘Valley of Shepherds’.

➥ Upon arrival, explore the enchanting Betaab Valley, a location famed for its lush meadows and clear streams, named after the Bollywood movie ‘Betaab’. Continue your adventure to the picturesque Aru Valley, known for its stunning landscapes and serene ambiance. Lastly, visit Chandanwari, the starting point of the annual Amarnath Yatra, where you can experience the pristine beauty of the snow-capped peaks and verdant valleys.

➥ Take a leisurely stroll along the banks of the glistening Lidder River, absorbing the tranquil atmosphere and the natural beauty that surrounds you. Capture the serene moments and let the refreshing mountain air rejuvenate your spirit.

➥ In the evening, choose to either return to Srinagar or stay overnight in Pahalgam to further immerse yourself in its serene charm.

DAY 5

Srinagar – Pahalgam Day Trip

➥ Begin your day with a visit to the local market in Pahalgam, where you can browse through a variety of traditional crafts and local produce. Enjoy the vibrant atmosphere and perhaps pick up a souvenir or two.

➥ Next, visit the ancient Mamleshwar Temple, a revered site dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple’s historical significance and tranquil setting offer a perfect spot for reflection and prayer.

➥ For those who love nature, embark on a nature walk or a trek in the surrounding hills. The lush greenery, the chirping of birds, and the panoramic views will make this a memorable experience.

➥ Spend the rest of your day relaxing and soaking in the peaceful ambiance of Pahalgam, letting the serenity of the place leave a lasting impression on your heart

DAY 6

Pahalgam to Srinagar – Houseboat Experience & Shikara Ride

➥ Start your day with a leisurely morning in Pahalgam, giving yourself the opportunity to explore any attractions you might have missed or simply to bask in the serene surroundings one last time.

➥ By late morning, set out on a scenic drive back to Srinagar. Upon arrival, check into a charming houseboat in the afternoon, where you can experience the unique and tranquil ambiance of living on the water.

➥ Spend your afternoon relaxing on the houseboat, soaking in the picturesque views of Dal Lake and the surrounding mountains.

➥ In the evening, embark on a delightful Shikara ride. Glide across the tranquil waters of Dal Lake, pass through the vibrant floating markets, and take in the breathtaking sunset over the horizon. Let the gentle rhythm of the boat and the beauty of the surroundings create an unforgettable end to your day.

DAY 7

Departure from Srinagar

➥ After breakfast, check out from your hotel or houseboat and bid farewell to the picturesque city of Srinagar.

➥ If time permits, indulge in some last-minute shopping for souvenirs such as traditional handicrafts, Pashmina shawls, or aromatic spices at local markets.

➥ Our representative will escort you to Srinagar International Airport for your onward journey, concluding this unforgettable getaway filled with mesmerizing landscapes and cherished memories.

DAY 3

Transfer from Pattaya – Enroute Sri Racha Tiger Zoo

  • It boasts to contain the largest number of Bengal tigers, bred successfully by the farm itself. Besides, the zoo has several other animals such as crocodiles, ostriches, single hump camels, kangaroos and an aviary of exotic birds. Exciting shows are Crocodile wrestling, Kenya aerobics, Amazing Circus and Pig Racing. Starting five years ago Sri Racha Tiger Zoo became the biggest zoo of its kind in the entire world providing a home for over 200 of this big and powerful animals. Set up in a beautiful natural atmosphere approximately 30 km outside of Pattaya the zoo is undoubtedly one of the best day attractions in the Eastern Seaboard. While tigers are the main reason for the majority of the visitors there are many other exciting attractions such as – Scorpion Queen, a young lady with plenty of poisonous scorpions on her clothing, Crocodile eggs breaking festival (May to August), Amazing Circus and crocodile wrestling show Pig racing The Happy Family, the friendship between a tiger, a pig and a dog, living together making it a must to be seen place. As usual for a zoo you will find many animals such as camels, deer, wallabies, elephants, Peruvian guinea pigs and more than 90,000 crocodiles. If you like you are able to touch and feed some of those animals ( Buy tickets for feeding directly) and have your pictures taken.

    Highlights
  • Visit Sriracha Tiger Zoo, with 10,000 crocodiles, 200 tigers
  • Watch all the animal programs available in the zoo

DAY 4

Grand Palace & Emerald Buddha – Dinner Cruise on Chao Phraya River

  • This tour includes the most beautiful place in Bangkok: Wat Traimit with its invaluable Golden Buddha (5 1⁄2 tons of solid gold) Wat Po, the most extensive temple in Bangkok, with its colossal Reclining Buddha and the Chedis of the Kings. The Grand Palace is one of the most beautiful samples of an ancient Siamese court. It used to be the residence of the Kings of Thailand. Here you will see palaces which were used for different occasions: the Funeral Palace, the Reception Palace, the Throne Hall, the Coronation Hall, the Royal Guest House, and the beautiful Emerald Buddha Temple. This tour includes Thonburi is the western part of Bangkok, situated on the right side of the Chao Phya River. Its many canals had originally given Bangkok the name “Venice of the East.” Passing by boat along the canals gives you a firsthand impression of the scenic life along Bangkok’s waterways. Also visit the Royal Barges and the Temple of Dawn. Finishing the tour will be a visit to the famous jewelry store Gems Gallery.
  • A Bangkok river cruise is a luxurious way to view main attractions.Pass by an illuminated Bangkok, which is simply mesmerizing with its glimmering lights that dance on the water’s surface, and see ancient temples such as Wat Arun, known also as the Temple of Dawn. Chao Phraya is as iconic to Bangkok as the Seine is to Paris or the Thames is to London and it winds its way by the city’s best sights.During your Chao Phraya River dinner cruise, feast on a buffet of authentic Thai dishes while watching the lit-up city skyline pass by.Settle down to your meal as a violinist and saxophonist pour their souls into weaving tunes that lift your spirit and set the mood for your evening.

    Highlights
  • Serene river cruise encompassing rich Thai royal history.

  • Delicious & tasty buffet with international delicacies
  • Jovial mood on board with music arrangement from a live band
  • Lively & upbeat local & international songs to dance
